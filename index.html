ABOUT US

We are professionals that specialize in real estate sales, purchases and mortgages, and have been in the industry for over 40 years. At some point in our lives we all must realize that a success not shared is no success at all. There is no greater and higher purpose than one that is outside of one’s self. We have first-hand witnessed the challenges that our veterans experience, along with their families, as they come back to the lives they once left to serve our country, many of them suffering from physical and mental conditions. We came upon the idea of using our network, knowledge and skills in the industry by creating this service as a means of giving back to our community, but most especially our veterans.

Inspired by our own veteran family and friends, we seek to give back by using our expertise in various industries to help veterans and their families connect with honest professionals and businesses by creating our own network of people who share our core values of INTEGRITY & SERVICE. Our vision is a better quality of life for our heroes and their families and a rewarding journey for our professionals who are truly in the business of SERVICE!
